FOUNDER RESOLUTION  
Authorization of Founder Working‑Capital Loan  
GitDigital Products LLC

WHEREAS, GitDigital Products LLC (“Company”) requires working capital  
for hardware, software, and operational runway;

WHEREAS, the Founder, Richard Duane Kindler (“Founder”), is willing to  
provide such capital through a non‑interest‑bearing, flexible,  
founder‑friendly loan;

THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED THAT:

1. The Company is authorized to borrow funds from the Founder under the  
   terms of the Promissory Note titled “Founder Working‑Capital Loan.”

2. The loan shall be classified as founder‑provided working capital and  
   recorded as a liability on the Company’s books.

3. The loan shall be non‑interest‑bearing, with no fixed maturity date,  
   and repayable only when the Company is cash‑flow positive.

4. The Company authorizes the Founder to forgive all or part of the loan  
   based on milestones or strategic value delivered.

5. The Company authorizes the Founder to report the loan as a positive  
   tradeline to business credit bureaus.

6. The Company authorizes the creation and maintenance of a Loan Ledger  
   documenting all disbursements, repayments, and forgiveness events.

APPROVED AND ADOPTED by the undersigned Founder of  
GitDigital Products LLC.

_  
Richard Duane Kindler  
Founder, GitDigital Products LLC
